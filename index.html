Translation Utils

These are utilities for Undercards translators, normal users don't need them.

Outdation tool, adds two buttons to the translation interface CHECK and COMPL. (Don't forget to choose your language)

CHECK automatically assigns an OUTDATED translation to translations containing "quest-s".

COMPL automatically confirms translations containing "quest-s". (in Waiting)

What Prereviewer does is that when you hover over a card link, and click SHIFT, then it will show you what the card will look like after applying the translation. Site utilities are used, so all line breaks, font size, etc., will look as shown in the script.
